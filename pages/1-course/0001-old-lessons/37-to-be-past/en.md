# 37. Verb to be past tense forms

## to be past tense forms {new}

I was
you were
he was
she was
it was
we were
they were

## negative {new}

was not, wasn't
were not, weren't

## New words {new}

to finish
to return
to open
to close
to enjoy

## Past forms of alredy known verbs {new}

wake up, woke up
eat, ate
drink, drank 
buy, bought 
then

## Story 1: Present Simple

My friend is a doctor.
He is smart and good.
He works in a big hospital.
He helps people.
He loves his work.
He does not like his work? No, he loves it.
He is tall and strong.
He has a good family.
His wife is a teacher.
She isn't a doctor.
They have two children.
They live in a beautiful house.
We often meet on weekends.
I finish my work early on Fridays.
My brother finishes his work late.
I return home and open the door.
I close the door behind me.
I enjoy my quiet room.
Do you enjoy your work?
Yes, I enjoy it.
I do not want new work.
Does he work on Sundays?
No, he doesn't work on Sundays.

## Story 2: Present Continuous

I am in the park now.
The sun is hot.
The sky is blue.
It isn't cold today.
Many people are here.
Children are playing.
They are running and jumping.
They aren't sitting.
A boy is on a bicycle.
A girl is reading a book.
She is sitting outside.
We are looking at the sky.
Are you enjoying the park?
Yes, I am enjoying it.
I am not working today.
My friend is writing a message.
We are not sleeping.
We are eating lunch.
Are they drinking tea?
No, they are drinking coffee.

## Story 3: Future Simple

I will travel to France next week.
My friends will go with me.
We will visit the country.
We will see the city.
I will buy some clothes there.
It will be good.
Will you come with us?
No, I won't come with you.
We will stay in a house.
The house will be big and clean.
We will cook French food.
I hope the weather will be good.
We will not work there.
We will enjoy our trip.
Will it be expensive?
No, it won't be expensive.
I will finish my plan tomorrow.
I will return next month.
I will open a new book.
I will close my computer.
It will be a good time.

## Story 4: Past Simple

I woke up early yesterday.
It was seven o'clock.
I did not sleep late.
I was in the bathroom.
I ate breakfast with my family.
We had tea and bread.
We didn't have coffee.
Then I went to work.
I worked in an office.
I wasn't at home.
The work was fast and hard.
My friend spoke to me at lunch.
We ate lunch at a restaurant.
I did my work to six.
Then I went home.
I didn't finish late.
I was tired but happy.
Were you tired yesterday?
Yes, I was tired.
We had a party on Saturday.
It was a party for my brother.
Many friends came to our house.
They gave him things.
My mother made fish and meat.
She didn't make breakfast.
We drank tea and water.
We didn't drink coffee.
We played and were happy.
I spoke with my friends.
Did you enjoy the party?
Yes, it was good.
We went to sleep late.
Did you go home early?
No, I didn't go home early.
We weren't sad.
They weren't at school.
My friend lived in China.
He studied Chinese.
He worked in a shop.
He played with his friends.
She bought milk and eggs.
She didn't buy meat.
She saw her friend.
Then she took the food.
Did she buy any bread?
No, she didn't buy the bread.
