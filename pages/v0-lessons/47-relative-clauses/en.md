# 47. The man who lives here (Relative Clauses)

## New words {new}

who
that
which
person
people
neighbor
kind
angry

## Who / That (for people)

He is the person who helped me.
I know the man who lives here.
She is the woman who called you.
People who work here are busy.
Do you know the girl who is dancing?
This is the boy who broke the window.
I like people who are kind.
The neighbor who lives in this house is old.
He is the student who studies English.
She is the person that I love.
The people that you see are my friends.
I don't like people who are angry.
He is the doctor who helped my father.
This is the neighbor who has a dog.
She is the teacher who teaches us.

## Which / That (for things)

This is the book which I read.
I have a car which is red.
The house which stands here is old.
This is the phone which I bought.
I like the dress which she wears.
The movie which we watched was good.
This is the photo that I took.
The question that you asked is difficult.
I ate the cake which was on the table.
The letter which came today is for you.
This is the key that opens the door.
The water which is in the glass is cold.
I found the money which I lost.
The bus which goes to the city is late.
This is the computer that I use.
