# 48. The person who can help me (Relative Clauses)

person
neighbor
letter
photo
game
bus
village
funny

comfortable

## Who / That

This is the person who can help me.
I know the person who can work with you.
The neighbor who has a dog is old.
The woman who can live in this house is my friend.
The man who can work in the school is my teacher.
The boy who can play with my son is funny.
The girl who can study English is my friend.
I like the person who can help me.
This is the neighbor who has a cat.
The person who can call you is here.

## Which / That

This is the letter which I can read.
The photo which you have is new.
The game which we play is interesting.
This is the bus which can go to the park.
The chair which is comfortable is my chair.
The house which is in the village is old.
The village which I live in is small.
This is the photo that I like.
I have a letter which is for you.
The book which I read is good.
