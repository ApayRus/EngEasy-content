## Basic plural rules

### Most nouns: add -s {new}

bʊk, bʊks
pen, penz
fəʊn, fəʊnz
ˈteɪbəl, ˈteɪbəlz

### Nouns ending in -s, -ss, -x, -ch, -sh: add -es {new}

ɡlɑːs, ˈɡlɑːsɪz
bɒks, ˈbɒksɪz
wɒtʃ, ˈwɒtʃɪz
dɪʃ, ˈdɪʃɪz

### Nouns ending in consonant + y: change y to i and add -es {new}

ˈkʌntri, ˈkʌntriz
ˈfæməli, ˈfæməliz
