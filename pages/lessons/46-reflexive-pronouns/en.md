# 46. I did it myself (Reflexive Pronouns)

## New words {new}

myself
yourself
yourselves
himself
herself
itself
ourselves
themselves
mirror
cut, cut, cut
hurt, hurt, hurt

## Singular (Myself, Yourself, Himself, Herself, Itself)

I saw myself in the mirror.
She bought herself a new coat.
He hurt himself.
Do it yourself.
The cat sees itself in the mirror.
I cut myself.
She often talks to herself.
He made dinner himself.
Did you hurt yourself?
I myself saw it.
She paid for herself.
He made this house himself.
Don't cut yourself!
I want to do it myself.
She looks at herself.

## Plural (Ourselves, Yourselves, Themselves)

We had a good time at the party.
They bought themselves a car.
Help yourselves to the food.
We made this house ourselves.
They hurt themselves.
We saw ourselves in the mirror.
Did you have a good time?
They can do it themselves.
We paid for ourselves.
They talk to themselves.
We cut ourselves.
Help yourselves!
They made dinner themselves.
We wash ourselves.
They live here themselves.
