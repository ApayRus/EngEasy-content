# 38. Future in the Past

## New words {new}

to walk
sleep, slept 
run, ran 
sing, sang 
find, found
leave, left
think, thought
know, knew
get, got
become, became
will, would 
well

## Future in the Past

I knew he would come.
She thought I would be late.
We knew they would leave early.
He thought it would be cold.
I knew she would get the message.
They thought we would find the house.
I thought you would sleep well.
She knew I would become a teacher.
We thought the children would run in the park.
He knew I would sing at the party.


## Story 1: Yesterday Morning

I woke up early yesterday.
It was a beautiful morning.
The sun was hot and the sky was blue.
I ate breakfast with my family.
We had bread and eggs.
Then I left home.
I walked to the park.
The park was big and green.
Many people were there.
I saw children playing.
They were running and jumping.
I thought about my family.
I knew I had good friends.
I thought they would come to the park later.
A dog came to me.
It was small and brown.
The dog ran to the water.
I walked around the park.
I found a quiet river.
The water was clean and blue.
I didn't want to leave.
But I got hungry.
I left the park at twelve o'clock.
I walked home.
My mother made lunch for me.
She made fish and meat.
It was good.
I was happy.

## Story 2: Last Monday

Last Monday was a difficult day.
I was tired and sad in the morning.
I didn't want to go to work.
But I left home at eight o'clock.
I walked to work.
I got to the office late.
I worked hard.
I didn't eat lunch.
I was hungry and weak.
My friend made me coffee.
She knew I was tired.
I was sad.
At six o'clock I finished my work.
I left the office.
I walked home.
I thought about my day.
I knew tomorrow would be better.
I thought I would sleep well.
I got home at seven o'clock.
My brother was there.
He made dinner for me.
He made bread and meat.
We ate.
I was better.
We talked about our week.
Then I went to sleep early.
I slept well.

## Story 3: A New Friend

I was in the city last Saturday.
I walked around the streets.
I looked at the shops and buildings.
I saw many people.
Some were busy, some were free.
I was sad.
I wanted to meet a friend.
I went to a restaurant.
I bought coffee.
A girl came to my table.
She was from Russia.
I was from my country.
We talked about our countries.
She knew some English.
I was happy.
Some children ran in the park.
We became friends.
We left the restaurant.
We walked to the park.
We found the river.
She had her phone.
She showed me her family.
I showed her my family.
We talked for hours.
We sang a song.
I was happy.
At six o'clock she left.
But she gave me her number.
I thought about her.
I knew I found a good friend.
I thought we would meet later.
I knew she would call me.

Yes, they ran in the park.
