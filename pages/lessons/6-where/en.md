# 6. Where's the phone?

## Question words {new|questionWord}

where

## Short forms {new|contraction}

where's = where is

## Articles {new|article}

the

## Prepositions {new|preposition}

on
in
under

## Nouns {new|noun}

box
bag
book
key
phone

## Questions

Where is the phone?
Where's the phone?
The phone is on the table.

Where's the key?
The key is in the bag.

Where's the book?
The book is on the chair.

Where's the box?
The box is under the table.
