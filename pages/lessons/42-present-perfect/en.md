# 42. I have just done it (Present Perfect)

## Time markers {new}

just
already
yet
ever
recently

## Verbs (3 forms) {new}

be, was, been
do, did, done
see, saw, seen
go, went, gone
come, came, come
send, sent, sent
pay, paid, paid
write, wrote, written
eat, ate, eaten
give, gave, given
lose, lost, lost

## Present Perfect vs Past Simple

I have just lost my key.
I lost my key yesterday.
I have seen him recently.
I saw him five minutes ago.
She has written a book.
She wrote a book last year.
We have finished the work.
We finished the work at five o'clock.
He has lived in Russia.
He lived in Russia in 2000.
They have gone to Spain.
They went to Spain last month.
I have not seen him yet.
I did not see him yesterday.
Have you ever been to France?
Were you in France last year?
He has done it.
He did it yesterday.
She has paid the money.
She paid the money an hour ago.
We have eaten breakfast.
We ate breakfast in the morning.
