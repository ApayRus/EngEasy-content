# 36. Object pronouns

## Object pronouns {new}

me
you
him
her
it
us
them

## New words {new}

to love
to hear
to show
to understand

## Basic sentences

I see him.
He sees me.
We know them.
They know us.
She helps him.
He helps her.
I like it.
You like them.
We call her.
She calls us.
They hear me.
I hear them.
He loves her.
She loves him.
We visit them.
They visit us.
I understand you.
You understand me.
He needs it.
She wants it.

## Short answers

Do you know him?
Yes, I do.
No, I don't.

Does he like her?
Yes, he does.
No, he doesn't.

Is this for me?
Yes, it is.
No, it isn't.

Are you with them?
Yes, I am.
No, I'm not.

Will you call us?
Yes, I will.
No, I won't.

Did you see them?
Yes, I did.
No, I didn't.
