# 38. Past Continuous

## New words {new}

to walk
sleep, slept
run, ran
sing, sang
find, found
leave, left
think, thought
know, knew
get, got
become, became
well

## Past Continuous (Affirmative)

I was walking in the park.
He was sleeping in the bedroom.
She was running to school.
It was cold.
We were singing a song.
You were thinking about the question.
They were leaving the house.
I was getting tired.

## Past Continuous (Negative)

I wasn't walking in the city.
He wasn't sleeping on the sofa.
She wasn't running fast.
It wasn't hot.
We weren't singing well.
You weren't thinking about work.
They weren't leaving the room.
I wasn't getting hungry.

## Past Continuous (Questions)

Were you walking yesterday?
Yes, I was walking.
Was he sleeping in the morning?
No, he wasn't sleeping.
Was she running to the shop?
Yes, she was running.
Were they singing a song?
No, they weren't singing.
Were you thinking about me?
Yes, I was thinking about you.
Was it getting late?
No, it wasn't getting late.

## Comparison (Past Simple - Past Continuous)

I ran to the park.
I was running to the park.

She slept well.
She was sleeping well.

He wrote a message.
He was writing a message.

We ate dinner.
We were eating dinner.

They played with toys.
They were playing with toys.

## Story: A Day in the Park

Yesterday was a hot day.
The sun was yellow.
I was walking in the park.
I didn't walk fast.
I wasn't working.
I was happy.
I saw a girl.
Was she running?
No, she wasn't running.
She was walking near the river.
She was singing a song.
She sang a beautiful song.
Did she see me?
No, she didn't see me.
She was thinking about something.
Then she left her bag on a chair.
She left.
I found the bag.
I got it.
I ran to the girl.
I spoke to her.
She saw me.
I gave her the bag.
She became happy.
She thought she left it on the chair.
We spoke for a moment.
She wasn't busy.
We became friends.
It was a good day.
